Face to Face with Jesus

"Religion” portrays the image of what it defines as godliness...but has no power to back it up. It feeds on the concept of attaining right standing with God through self-effort while diminishing what Jesus has done for us.

It's essential you understand the differences between the old covenant of law and the new covenant of grace. Here's one of my favorite Scriptures: "For the law was given through Moses, but grace and truth came through Jesus Christ." —John 1:17 

The law was given through a servant...Grace and truth came through the Son.
